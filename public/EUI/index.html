Enter your DNA Samples as a CSV file. First, open up software like Google Sheets or Microsoft Excel. Then make two entries in the first row to make your title (I suggest to keep it as DNA and Effeciency but The title will be ignored). Next, enter all your inputs in the respective columns. Make sure all the DNA strings are of the same length. Effeciency must be a positive rational number. Next export it to a CSV file. To export to CSV , refer this for Ms-Excel and for Google sheets Click the File tab, Choose Download,Select the Comma-separated values (CSV) option. Repeat and upload for how many ever variations you have.
